Maple.fm
A MapleStory service for active players and merchants that displays information about the game's Free Market. The service was first launched in December of 2013.

  • Auto-updating market information for every MapleStory (Global) server viewable through browsers, tablets, and mobile (iOS & Android).
  • Detailed item information and average unique pricings per server.
  • Dedicated support and assistance.
